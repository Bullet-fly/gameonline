Monster Survivors

Survive the night in this intense action game where you battle waves of relentless monsters

About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an exhilarating action game that puts your survival skills to the ultimate test. As a lone survivor in a world overrun by monsters, you must use your wits and weapons to stay alive through increasingly challenging waves of enemies.

Game Features

  • Intense wave-based combat system
  • Diverse monster types with unique abilities
  • Progressive difficulty curve
  • Strategic gameplay mechanics
  • Responsive controls optimized for all devices

How to Play

Use your mouse or touch controls to aim and shoot at incoming monsters. Collect power-ups and upgrade your weapons to become stronger. Each wave brings new challenges and tougher enemies. Can you survive the night?
